GOLDEN RATIO

The golden ratio, also known as the divine proportion, golden mean, or golden section. In mathematics,two quantities are in the golden ratio if their ratio is the same as the ratio of their sum to the larger of the two quantities which equals to a Greek letter phi. Expressed algebraically, for quantities a and b with a > b > 0
